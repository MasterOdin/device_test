Instructions

Thank you for participating in this study. During it, you will be asked to point your phone in a number of directions and hit a button in the center of the screen to record the orientation of the phone at that time. Information collected will be your phone model, browser version, and the aforementioned coordinate data points. No identifying information will be collected.

When you are ready, hit the continue button below. You will see an instruction on the direction to point your phone above a button that says "Click". This instruction will get updated each time you press the button.

Any comments, questions, or concerns can be directed to Matthew Peveler (matthew.peveler@ibm.com).

Step

Place the phone flat in your hand and point it in front of you.
Rotate 90 degrees to your left, keeping the phone flat.
Rotate another 90 degrees to your left, 180 degrees from starting position.
Rotate another 90 degrees to your left, 90 degrees to the right of the starting position.
Rotate a final 90 degrees to your left, back to the starting position.
Rotate 90 degrees to your right, keeping the phone flat.
Rotate another 90 degrees to your right, 180 degrees from the starting position.
Rotate another 90 degrees to your right, 90 degrees to the left of the starting position.
Rotate a final 90 degrees to your right, back to the starting position.
Rotate the phone 90 degrees onto its side, so that the screen is pointing to the left.
Rotate the phone 90 more degrees, so its screen is facing downward.
Rotate the phone another 90 degrees, so the screen is pointing to the right.
Rotate the phone back to its original starting poition.
Rotate the phone 90 degrees onto its side, so that the screen is pointing to the left.
Rotate the phone 90 more degrees, so its screen is facing downward.
Rotate the phone another 90 degrees, so the screen is pointing to the right.
Rotate the phone back to its original poition.
Rotate the phone so that it is pointing upwards with the screen facing you.
Rotate the phone 90 degrees to the left so that it is pointing to the left with the screen facing you.
Rotate the phone so that it is pointing at the ground.
Rotate the phone another 90 degrees so that it is pointing to the left.
Rotate the phone so that it is pointing upwards with the screen facing you.
Rotate the phone 90 degrees to the right so that it is pointing to the right with the screen facing you.
Rotate the phone so that it is pointing at the ground.
Rotate the phone 90 degrees so that it is pointing to the left.
